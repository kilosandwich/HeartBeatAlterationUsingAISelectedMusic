Before you start using the application

You will need to set your Garmin device to broadcast mode

  • 1.On the heart rate widget hold up
  • 2.Select options and go to broadcast heart rate
  • 3.Pair your device to your Garmin Ant+ device
    •

    Reference Guide

  • Navigation Bar - The navbar can take you to the user-guide.
  • Target Heart Rate - The target heart rate will be set for the user at 70.
  • +/- - The up and down arrows move in intervals of 10. They allow the user to change the heart rate.
  • Start - The start button is what the user presses, when they are ready to get their music choice.
  • Audio player - The audio player will play the song and display the song name.
    •

    Advanced Features

  • This the advanced features part of our web application. Here you can choose an approach path, to alter your heart rate. Depending on how you want to alter your heart rate you can either go slow or really fast.
  • You can also press the get resting Heart rate button, to get your live resting heart rate. Once you press the button, the resting heart rate will be displayed on screen and fill in the box.
